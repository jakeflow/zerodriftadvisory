About

Jake Muniak

I'm an IAM consultant and certified project manager with a background in enterprise SaaS delivery for regulated industries. I spent years managing complex implementations for life sciences and pharma clients - learning how to run discovery, manage stakeholders, and ship deliverables that actually get used.

I pivoted into identity and access management because I kept seeing the same problem: organizations know they need better access governance, but they don't have the in-house expertise or bandwidth to get it done. That's the gap I fill.

Zero Drift Advisory is built on a simple principle: no one should have access they don't actively need. I help organizations implement that principle without overcomplicating it.

Methodology

How we work

Every engagement follows a repeatable, structured methodology - whether you're 10 users or 500.

Discover

Structured questionnaire and stakeholder interviews to map your user population, platform landscape, and current access practices.

Assess

Full access matrix, risk register, and gap analysis against IAM best practices. Every finding is rated and prioritized.

Recommend

Phased remediation plan with clear owners, timelines, and deliverables. Designed for your ops team, not a security engineer.

Implement

Hands-on support configuring IdP policies, group structures, JML workflows, and governance cadences.
