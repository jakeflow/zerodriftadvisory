What we do

Practical identity governance for organizations that need it done right - not overengineered, not underscoped.

IAM Access Reviews

Comprehensive audit of who has access to what across your entire platform landscape. You get a full access matrix, risk register, and prioritized action plan.

JML Lifecycle Design

Joiner, Mover, Leaver processes that actually get followed. Checklists, role libraries, and offboarding workflows tailored to how your team operates.

IdP Configuration

Google Workspace, Okta, or Entra ID - configured correctly with SSO, MFA enforcement, and group-based access policies. No more manual provisioning chaos.

Governance Frameworks

Access review cadences, attestation processes, and policy documentation that satisfy compliance requirements without burying your ops team in overhead.

SSO Configuration

SAML and OIDC wiring so your team signs in once and gets access to everything they need. We handle the federation setup, attribute mapping, and testing across your SaaS stack so your users stop juggling passwords.

Okta & Entra Configuration

Platform-level IdP setup done right. Conditional access policies, group-based provisioning, MFA enforcement, and directory sync between Okta or Entra ID and the rest of your environment. Built to hand off cleanly to your internal team.
